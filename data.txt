Q.WHY IS MY WITHDRAWAL REQUEST FAILING?		
A.IF ACCOUNT NUMBER, BANK NAME, IFSC CODE, OR ACCOUNT HOLDER NAME IS INCORRECT,
THE SYSTEM CANNOT PROCESS THE TRANSFER.
SO THE WITHDRAWAL REQUEST GETS REJECTED / FAILED.

Q.WHY HAVE I NOT RECEIVED MY WITHDRAWAL YET?		
A.INCORRECT BANK DETAILS (ACCOUNT NUMBER / IFSC / NAME).
BANK COULD NOT PROCESS THE TRANSFER.
WITHDRAWAL IS DELAYED OR FAILED.

Q.HOW LONG DOES A WITHDRAWAL TAKE TO PROCESS?
A.FIRST, THE REQUEST IS REVIEWED AND APPROVED.
THEN IT IS SENT TO THE BANK FOR PROCESSING.
BANK PROCESSING TIME MAY VARY.

Q.CAN WITHDRAWAL FAIL DUE TO INCOMPLETE KYC?		
A.KYC VERIFICATION IS REQUIRED FOR WITHDRAWALS.
IF DOCUMENTS ARE MISSING, PENDING, OR NOT APPROVED,
THE SYSTEM WILL BLOCK OR REJECT THE WITHDRAWAL.

Q.CAN INCORRECT BANK DETAILS CAUSE WITHDRAWAL ISSUES?		
A.WRONG ACCOUNT NUMBER, IFSC CODE, OR BANK NAME.
MISMATCH IN ACCOUNT HOLDER NAME.
BANK CANNOT PROCESS THE TRANSFER.
